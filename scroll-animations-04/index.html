Stacking cards on scroll

Each card sticks at the top of the page. A staggered padding-top makes the older ones peek out from behind the newer ones. A view-timeline on the wrapper drives a scale animation, so older cards visually recede as you scroll past. This uses exit-crossing, which is when an element's leading edge leaves the viewport.

The whole trick

Two layered ideas: sticky cards with a cascading peek, plus one shared timeline that scales each card during its slice of the exit phase.
